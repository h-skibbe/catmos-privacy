Privacy Policy – Catmos Watch Face

This watch face does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Catmos Watch Face uses system-provided data such as time, battery level, step count, moon phase, and weather information via standard Wear OS complications. This data stays on the watch and is never shared.

This app is fully compliant with the Watch Face Format (WFF).

There is no internet access, no user tracking, and no third-party services involved.
